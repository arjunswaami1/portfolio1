Spider Quadruped

2 Months

Arduino, 3D Printing, Robotics, AI

Legged robots are more useful and efficient in
the uneven terrains when compared with wheeled
robots as they cannot climb the rocks and rough
surfaces easily. The legged robots like Quadruped
robots can climb the terrains like rocks, sludge,
incline and decline surfaces easily as their mechanised
legs are made for these surfaces. Quad Robots can be
made in all different shape and sizes. The first ever
quad robot was made in the shape of a dog. The
following Quadruped robot was made in the shape of a
spider. This is a basic prototype quadruped robot. These
robots can be controlled by Wi-Fi or Bluetooth and it
can be automated by Artificial Intelligence.
